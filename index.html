Hi, I'm Antoine

I'm a Computer-Science Ph.D. student interested in Semantic REST APIs, Linked Data and distributed architectures who try to make pragmatic things people can reuse. Also, sometimes I write articles and talk to colleagues or conferences.

The Research I Do

As a Computer-Science Ph.D. student

I focus my research in the field of distributed information systems towards two goals:

  • reducing the coupling between components exposing or consuming Web APIs
  • making the IS easier to query with no prior knowledge of its architecture

Therefore, I read a lot about REST APIs, the Semantic Web, Linked Data, Open Data, Linked Data Platform and Semantic REST APIs.
